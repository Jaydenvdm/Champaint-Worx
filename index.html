Frequently Asked Questions

Our premium epoxy flooring systems typically last 10-20 years with proper maintenance. Commercial installations may require recoating after 8-12 years depending on traffic levels. We offer a 10-year warranty on all residential installations.

While epoxy is UV-resistant, prolonged direct sunlight can cause fading over time. We recommend keeping epoxy tables under covered patios or using UV-protective sealants for full outdoor exposure. Our outdoor-rated epoxy formulations come with a 5-year warranty against yellowing and cracking.

Epoxy floors are incredibly low-maintenance. For daily cleaning, simply sweep or dust mop. For deeper cleaning, use a pH-neutral cleaner and warm water. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive tools. We recommend applying a maintenance coat every 3-5 years for high-traffic areas to keep your floor looking its best.

The timeline varies based on complexity, size, and our current project queue. Simple river tables typically take 4-6 weeks from design approval to completion. More intricate designs with embedded elements or custom pigments may take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

During our site evaluation, we'll assess your existing floor and recommend any necessary repairs or primers. The only surfaces we typically can't coat are carpet or flexible flooring materials.
